Our DLD figures (official project_number → developer chain) next to published 2024 benchmarks, so the team can verify each number against an external source.

Read the numbers carefully

Figures differ by scope: we count all registered sales (off-plan + ready/resale) for the full calendar year; several sources count off-plan only, or developer bookings, or a different window. Closeness (not exact equality) is the signal. Developer attribution covers ~49.0% of all sales — the share linked to a DLD-registered project (off-plan & newer builds are well covered; many older secondary resales carry no project link).
